2Description of Service

GeneGaze is a client-side genomic analysis application that processes raw DNA data files from services such as 23andMe, AncestryDNA, and other direct-to-consumer genetic testing providers.

The app provides educational insights about:

  • 1,000+ clinically significant health markers and genetic variants across 10 categories
  • Pharmacogenomic medication response profiles for 50+ drugs
  • Personalized nutrition and supplement guidance
  • Ancestry and population markers
  • Physical trait predictions (hair, skin, eyes, taste, body composition)
  • Side-by-side genome comparison (up to 3 files)
  • PDF report export and email sharing for healthcare providers

All data processing occurs entirely on your device. No genetic data is transmitted to any server.
